The Spirit Behind Hiking Tarija

Uncover the inspiration, dedication, and love for nature that shaped this space for hikers and explorers.

From Vision to Reality

Hiking Tarija was born from a dream first imagined in 1974 by my grandfather, who grew up in the quiet community of O'Connor, surrounded by valleys, open prairies, and the vast beauty of Tarija’s landscapes. He spent his youth climbing mountains, crossing rivers, and walking long distances simply to admire the breathtaking views that shaped his connection to nature.

Over the years, this love for exploration became a defining part of the Vega family. Each generation learned to appreciate the mountains not just as places to visit, but as spaces to grow, reflect, and understand the world around them. What began as a personal passion slowly transformed into a shared family tradition-one built on curiosity, resilience, and a deep respect for the land.

Today, Hiking Tarija carries that legacy forward. Our mission is to open the door for others to experience the same wonder my grandfather felt decades ago. Through carefully curated trails, thoughtful guides, and a commitment to preserving Tarija’s natural heritage, we strive to help every hiker discover the landscapes that shaped our story-and perhaps inspire new ones of their own.

Our Trail Philosophy

Respect for Nature

Empowering Local Communities
